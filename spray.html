On November 19th, the project will be premiered at the BEINGHUNTED. Space in Berlin with new artworks, paintings and a series of drawings, by FUTURA. ACRONYM® has provided a small number of their yet to be released J1A-GT jacket – an updated version of the legendary first ACRONYM® piece from 2002 – as well as a limited amount of the 3A-9TS bag. Each item will be customized/painted by FUTURA and available for purchase.

The MONTANA-CANS “FUTURA 2000 x ACRONYM®” as well as a limited edition BEINGHUNTED. T-shirt will be available during the evening as well as through the BEINGHUNTED. online shop afterwards.

Location

MONTANA-CANS, FUTURA, ACRONYM® and BEINGHUNTED are happy to welcome guests on November 19th from 18:00 – 22:00 at Wallstrasse 4, Berlin 10179.

For further information regarding the purchase of original artworks by FUTURA, serious inquiries should be made via the webform.
